About Resha

RESHA METAL FORMING established on 2020 at Coimbatore. This unit is promoted by one of the rich experienced engineering group of BARANI HYDRAULICS INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED, with a vision of becoming a key player in manufacturing of engineering components and sheet metal parts. Our organization have space of 10,000 sq.ft. and high quality CNC,VMC,VTL,HTL, Co2 Laser cutting and CNC Bending machines. In addition we have Trimos, CMM (CAREL-ZEISS) and other measuring instruments and gauges facility.

We believe in team work, continual improvement and strive to find opportunities for innovation. The company is constantly developing new products to satisfy customer requirements and set new goals and plans to meet.

Vision

It is the Vision of RESHA METAL FORMING that it is to be looked upon as not only a company that provides high quality products and services, but also a vital business and community leader to maintaining high standards and ethical conduct in its business performance.
